Club Dead in Tampa, Florida

Want to have a bone-chilling, hair-raising, spine-tingling, supernatural adventure? Then come to the Cuban Club in Tampa, Florida!


The History of the Cuban Club

There have been several deaths in the building. Because of this, the club has earned the nickname "Club Dead." An actor committed suicide by jumping off the stage. An enraged board member shot another board member in the face after a fight. A woman in a white dress and red high heels stalks the staircases. A little boy, Jimmy, drowned in the pool. Their restless spirits still haunt the building. Jimmy plays with balls people bring for him and has been known to mischievously tug on people's shirts.
